We explore the remnants of a Compact Binary System.
"Anything that seems to be in the edge of our predictions is most interesting." - Chris Belczynski

Such predictions are also applied for the cosmic collisions. Because of the stark mass imbalance the collision of two black holes generated gravitational waves at multiple frequencies, in a harmony Elvis Fans would recognise. The chord also confirms Einstein's theory of gravity or general relativity.

What is a CBS?
  • Interaction between two Black holes.
    Hover to know more

A binary black hole (BBH) is a system consisting of two black holes in close orbit around each other by dynamic processes and binary supermassive black holes believed to be a result of galactic mergers.

  • Interaction between Black Hole and Neutron Star
    Hover to know more

BH-NS binaries are believed to be formed as a result of two supernovae in a massive binary system,orbital separation decreases gradually due to the longterm gravitational radiation reaction.

  • Interaction between two neutron Stars
    Hover to know more

When two neutron stars orbit each other closely, they spiral inward as time passes due to gravitational radiation,they meet and their merger leads to the formation of either a more massive neutron star, or a black hole
